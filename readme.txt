== Description ==

PREVIEW: http://te-portfolio.editpage.ru

tePortfolio plugin will help to create a full-fledged portfolio on the site even for those people who have no programming skills. The plugin is written in such a way that you do not have to dig in a code, and its interface is simple enough even for a novice user of Word Press.

TePortfolio will perfectly fit the sites of various themes. The plugin can be used for sites about equipment and gadgets. It will perfectly fit for display of companys employees or for a travel blog for demonstration of amazing sights of our planet.

Convenient functional allows to post works portfolio on your site in just a few minutes and with numerous management possibilities:

*    4 variants of display of the portfolio;
*    5 colour schemes of the portfolio;
*    5 effects when pointing on images;
*    filtration of works according to categories;
*    adding of any quantity of images for each work;
*    images slider for each work;
*    viewing of full (increased) image;
*    adding of progress bars for each work;
*    adjustment of colour for each progress bar;
*    adding of social network buttons for each work in the portfolio;
*    the portfolio is completely adapted for mobile devices;
*    and many other pleasant little things


== Installation ==

Login on your site as administrator in order to install the plugin. Then follow the path "Plugins" -> "Add New".
Click "Upload" in menu and select tePortfolio.zip. Click "Install Now" and then "Activate Plugin"

Full version: http://te-portfolio.editpage.ru/docs/
